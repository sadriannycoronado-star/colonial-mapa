Hidden Corners of the Colonial Zone

Walking Directions

  1. Start at the Cathedral Side Entrance. This is our meeting point.
  2. Cross the street to Columbus Park. Walk about 1 minute to the statue.
  3. Walk to Arzobispo Meriño Street. It is just one corner away.
  4. Cross the street to the Royal Houses Museum. Less than 2 minutes walking.
  5. Walk one block to Las Damas Street. This is the final stop.

Total walking time: about 5 minutes.
